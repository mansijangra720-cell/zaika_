Our Story

About Zaika Cafe

Founded in 2021, Zaika Cafe was born from a passion for authentic Indian cuisine and a dream to bring the rich, diverse flavors of India to every table. Our name "Zaika" means "taste" in Hindi — and that's exactly what we deliver.

Every dish at Zaika is crafted with hand-ground spices, locally sourced ingredients, and generations-old family recipes. From the smoky tandoori flavors of the North to the aromatic curries of the South, our menu is a journey across India's culinary landscape.
